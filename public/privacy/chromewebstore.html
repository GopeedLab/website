Privacy Policy

Welcome to Gopeed extension! Before using our extension, please read the following privacy policy.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects your Cookies when detecting new download tasks. These Cookies are used for downloading tasks when invoking an external downloader.

We only use the collected Cookies for downloading tasks and solely for local transmission. We do not transmit this information to any external servers.

Protection of Information

We have implemented appropriate security measures to protect your Cookies from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
